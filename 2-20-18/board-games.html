Board Games

What is a Board Game?

A board game is a tabletop game that usually involves counters or pieces that or moved on board according to a certain set of rules. Some games are soley based on luck while other games are based on skill. To play a board game you need at least 2 people and they are great for fun with friends and family.

What is my Favorite Board Game?

My favorite board game is Monopoly. Monopoly is usually played with 2-4 people and is a great game to play with friends and family. It involves the perfect combination of skill and luck so every time you play it is a little different. Here are three reasons why Monopoly is my favorite game:

  • Many people can play at once
  • There is enough skill involved that you can become good at the game
  • Players get very into it which makes it very competitive
